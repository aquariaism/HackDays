Farmer Producer Organizations (FPO)

Empowering Farmers through Collective Strength

FPOs are organizations formed by farmers to improve access to markets, better pricing, and collective bargaining power. Here, you can find details about various FPOs in your region.

  • Benefits: Better market access, cost reduction, higher bargaining power.
  • Eligibility: Open to all small and marginal farmers.
  • Government Support: Various schemes available for financial assistance.
