Soil Health Card Scheme

Description: Provides soil health cards to farmers.

Eligibility: All farmers.

Benefits: Recommendations for nutrient management.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Description: Crop insurance scheme for farmers.

Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops.

Benefits: Financial support in case of crop failure.

National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

Description: Online trading platform for agricultural products.

Eligibility: Farmers, traders, and buyers.

Benefits: Better price discovery and access to markets.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Description: Aims to improve irrigation facilities.

Eligibility: All farmers.

Benefits: Increased agricultural productivity.
